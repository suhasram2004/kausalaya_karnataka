# Kausalaya Karnataka

> Empowering Youth Through Skills and Entrepreneurship

## Overview

Kausalaya Karnataka is a comprehensive initiative designed to transform the employability landscape in Karnataka. The program bridges the gap between industry needs and available workforce skills, while promoting entrepreneurship and self-employment opportunities for youth across the state.

## 🎯 Main Objectives

- **Improve Employability**: Enhance job-readiness and professional capabilities among youth
- **Industry-Skill Gap Bridge**: Align training programs with current industry requirements and expectations
- **Promote Entrepreneurship**: Encourage self-employment and startup ventures
- **Industry Recognition**: Provide certifications that are valued and recognized across industries
- **Youth Empowerment**: Foster economic independence and career growth

## 👥 Target Beneficiaries

Youth aged approximately **18 to 35 years** (eligibility may vary by course)

## 🚀 Key Features

- Industry-recognized certifications
- Skill development programs aligned with market demands
- Entrepreneurship support and mentorship
- Self-employment opportunities
- Career guidance and placement assistance
